About the Network

The Staffordshire, Shropshire & Black Country Newborn and Maternity Networkâ€™s philosophy is that babies and their families who need additional help get the best care possible at the right time, in the right place and by a team of practitioners who are skilled and competent to care for the range of needs they may have.

All the units in the Network work in partnership so that babies can be cared for by the appropriate staff in the most appropriate unit as close to home as possible. Every member of staff within each unit will try to give the best care possible for your baby and family.

Neonatal intensive care is a very specialised service, and sometimes it may be necessary to transfer your baby to another unit to allow another baby to receive intensive care.

All the staff throughout the Network have collaborated on the information in this app to give parents as much information as possible about the individual units.

We would be very grateful for any feedback that you could give us about how we could improve our services for future babies and families.
